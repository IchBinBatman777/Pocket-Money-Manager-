# 💰 Pocket Money Manager

**Set a budget. Track every rupee. Never overspend again.**

A slick, zero-setup web app that helps you stay on top of your daily spending — no sign-up, no backend, just open and go. 🚀

🔗 **[Try it live →](#)** *https://ichbinbatman777.github.io/Pocket-Money-Manager-/*

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎯 **Set a daily budget** — updates instantly across the app
- ➕ **Add expenses** with a slick amount slider (₹5 steps)
- 📲 **Auto-add from UPI SMS** — just paste your payment SMS, and the amount + merchant get pulled out automatically
- 📊 **Live progress bar** that shifts 🟢 → 🟡 → 🔴 as you approach your limit
- ⚠️ **Smart alerts** when you're close to or over budget
- ✅ **Tap to mark done**, or delete expenses in one click
- 💾 **Auto-saves** everything in your browser — pick up right where you left off
- 📱 **Fully responsive** — looks great on phone or desktop

## 🚀 Getting Started

**Run it instantly:**
```bash
# just open index.html in your browser — that's it!
```

**Or serve it locally:**
```bash
python3 -m http.server 8000
```

**Deploy it for free in seconds:**
- 🟢 [Netlify Drop](https://app.netlify.com/drop) — drag, drop, done
- ⚫ GitHub Pages — enable it under Settings → Pages

---

## 📖 How to Use

1. 💵 Enter today's budget → **Save Budget**
2. 🛍️ Add an expense manually, or 📲 paste a UPI SMS to auto-add it
3. 👀 Watch your summary, progress bar & alerts update live
4. ✅ Tap an expense to mark it done, or 🗑️ delete it

---

## ⚠️ Good to Know

- Data lives in your browser (`localStorage`) — it won't sync across devices *yet*
- UPI SMS parsing is best-effort and may occasionally need a quick tweak to the merchant name
